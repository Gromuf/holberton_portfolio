About

I wanted to create a project for my school exam that incorporates both full-stack development and video games. Next year, I will have to choose between these two fields, both of which I enjoy. Since I haven't made my decision yet, I decided to develop a project that combines them. This game is an opportunity for me to explore these technologies and enhance my skills before making my choice.

Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
